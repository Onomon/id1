The color palette is a very muted one, outside of the maroon and used in certain places like the pull quote at the beginning of the page and the footer at the end there's, very little color outside of the blue of the sly in certain photos. other colors used were eggshell whites and different tones of grey to lead the eye and make things stand out.

  3. What role do the visuals play? When and how are visuals used?

  4. How are sections separated? What are the visual cues?
